# CONTENT_GUIDE.md

# Content & Copywriting Guidelines

Version 1.0

---

# Overview

This document defines the writing style,
tone of voice,
storytelling,
and content structure
for the digital wedding invitation.

Every sentence should feel
warm,
elegant,
romantic,
respectful,
and timeless.

The invitation should tell a story,
not simply display information.

---

# Writing Philosophy

The invitation is not an advertisement.

It is a personal story shared with family and friends.

Every section should create emotion.

---

# Tone of Voice

Primary

- Elegant
- Warm
- Romantic
- Respectful
- Calm

Secondary

- Modern
- Luxury
- Emotional
- Personal

Avoid

- Overly formal
- Overly casual
- Marketing language
- Excessive emojis
- Slang

---

# Languages

Primary

Bahasa Indonesia

Secondary

English

Future

Japanese

Arabic

Chinese

All content should support localization.

---

# Greeting

Example

Assalamu'alaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh

or

Dengan penuh rasa syukur,
kami mengundang Bapak/Ibu/Saudara/i
untuk hadir dalam hari bahagia kami.

Greeting should feel sincere.

---

# Guest Name

Always personalize.

Example

Kepada Yth.

Bapak/Ibu/Saudara/i

{{guest}}

If guest name is unavailable

Display

Tamu Undangan

---

# Couple Introduction

Introduce both bride and groom equally.

Include

Photo

Parents

Short Biography

Optional Occupation

Instagram

---

# Love Story

Tell the story naturally.

Suggested structure

First Meeting

↓

Friendship

↓

Relationship

↓

Engagement

↓

Marriage

Do not exaggerate.

---

# Wedding Quote

Allowed

Quran Verse

Bible Verse

Poetry

Traditional Javanese Philosophy

Love Quotes

Keep quotations concise.

---

# Event Information

Present clearly.

Include

Date

Time

Location

Dress Code (optional)

Maps

Calendar

---

# RSVP Text

Friendly

Simple

Clear

Example

Kehadiran Anda merupakan kebahagiaan bagi kami.

Mohon konfirmasi kehadiran melalui formulir berikut.

---

# Gift Section

Avoid sounding transactional.

Example

Doa restu Anda adalah hadiah terindah.

Namun apabila ingin memberikan tanda kasih,
Anda dapat menggunakan informasi berikut.

---

# Gallery

No caption required.

Let the photos tell the story.

---

# Video

Short cinematic description only.

Avoid long explanations.

---

# Closing

Thank guests sincerely.

Example

Merupakan suatu kehormatan
dan kebahagiaan bagi kami
apabila Bapak/Ibu/Saudara/i
berkenan hadir.

Terima kasih atas doa dan restunya.

---

# Religious Content

Support

Islam

Christian

Catholic

Hindu

Buddhist

General

Religious content should be configurable.

Never hardcode one religion.

---

# Javanese Elements

Encourage subtle cultural expressions.

Examples

- Pepatah Jawa
- Filosofi kehidupan
- Nilai kekeluargaan
- Kesopanan
- Gotong royong

Avoid excessive traditional wording that reduces readability.

---

# Typography Rules

Headings

Short

Elegant

Body

Readable

Comfortable

Quotes

Centered

Highlighted

---

# Capitalization

Use sentence case.

Avoid ALL CAPS except for decorative titles.

---

# Emoji

Use sparingly.

Recommended

💍

🌸

🤍

Avoid excessive emoji usage.

# Date Format

English

Saturday, October 10, 2026

# Time Format

24-hour format

13.30 WIB

# Address

City

# Button Labels

Use concise labels.

# Error Messages

Friendly

Example

Maaf,
terjadi kesalahan.

Silakan coba beberapa saat lagi.

---

# Empty States

Example

Belum ada ucapan.

Jadilah tamu pertama
yang memberikan doa dan harapan.

---

# Accessibility

Use readable language.

Avoid long paragraphs.

Keep sentence length comfortable.

---

# SEO Writing

Titles

Below 60 characters.

Descriptions

Below 160 characters.

Natural wording.

No keyword stuffing.

# Final Principle

Every word should make the guest feel
personally welcomed,
emotionally connected,
and honored to be part of the celebration.

The invitation should read
like a heartfelt letter,
not a generic website.
